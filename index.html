My name is Max Cohen and I am professional librarian with a focus on technology integration and usage in libraries. I am currently based in the Boston area but I am more than willing to travel.

I believe rather firmly that in order to succeed in providing the best possible service to patrons libraries need to embrace technology in all of it's forms. My aim as a librarian is to help patrons understand the resources that are available to them and to help my fellow librarians meet and exceed their technological goals.
